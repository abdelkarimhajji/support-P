Privacy Policy for Our Apps

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application our app, developed by Abdelkarim Hajji.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy first. Our applications collect the minimum amount of data required for functionality:

  • For Games: We may store local game progress and scores on your device.
  • For Network Utilities: We do NOT log your browsing history, traffic destination, or data content. We only process the technical connection data required to establish the secure tunnel.
  • For General Tools: We store your user preferences locally on your device to improve your experience.

2. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to improve our app experience:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements.
  • Firebase: For analytics and crash reporting (to help us fix bugs).

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. For our network tools, we use strong encryption to ensure your connection remains private and secure.
